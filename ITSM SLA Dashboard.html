Power BI SLA Compliance Dashboard

A Power BI dashboard analyzing SLA compliance in IT Service Management. Highlights SLA gaps by department, ticket priority, and region, enabling leaders to track service quality and operational performance.

Goal

Provide a single, executive-ready dashboard to monitor ITSM service quality by tracking SLA performance and identifying where breaches are occurring across teams, priorities, and regions.

Key insights available

  • KPI cards: total tickets, SLA performance, SLA target %.
  • Breaches by priority: high / medium / low ticket SLA violations.
  • Trend analysis: SLA % vs target across 17 months, highlighting missed periods.
  • Geo view: SLA compliance by country with clear “met vs missed” indicators.
  • Ticket types: incidents, problems, changes, requests.
  • Calendar heatmap: distribution + compliance across days and months.

Interactivity

Includes a custom department sidebar filter with icons for CXC, HR, IT Support, and Sales, enabling quick comparisons without leaving the main view.

What I built

  • Designed the dashboard layout and KPI structure for fast executive scanning.
  • Built multi-level drilldowns (priority, ticket type, region, department).
  • Created trend + heatmap views for operational monitoring over time.
  • Enabled interactive filtering to isolate SLA issues by team and geography.

Quick Facts

  • Category: BI / Analytics
  • Tool: Power BI
  • Domain: ITSM / SLA Monitoring
  • Time Window: 17 months
  • Dimensions: Department, Priority, Region
  • Views: KPIs, Trends, Geo, Heatmap
